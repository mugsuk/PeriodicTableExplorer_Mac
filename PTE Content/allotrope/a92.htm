Allotropes of Uranium
alpha (orthorhombic) Uranium [ U ]
Stable up to 940.84K (667.7°C).
beta (tetragonal) Uranium [ U ]
Stable from 940.84K (667.7°C) to 1047.94K (774.8°C)
gamma (body-centred cubic) Uranium [ U ]
Stable from 1047.94K (774.8°C) to its melting point. This is the most malleable and ductile state.
