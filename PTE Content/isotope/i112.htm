Isotopes of Copernicium
Notes
Like all synthetic elements, copernicium has no stable isotopes. The first isotope to be synthesized was 277Cn in 1996. There are 6 known radioisotopes and 2 possible isomers. The longest-lived isotope is 285Cn with a half-life of 29 seconds.
