Isotopes of Neptunium
Notes
Neptunium has no stable isotopes.

237Np [144 neutrons]
Abundance: synthetic
Half life: 2.144 x 106 years
Decay Energy: 4.959 MeV
Decays to 232Pa.
237Np is irradiated with neutrons to create 238Pu, a rare and valuable isotope for spacecraft and military applications. 237Np will capture a neutron to form 238Np and beta decay with a half life of two days to 238Pu.
