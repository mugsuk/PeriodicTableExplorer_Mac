Isotopes of Dubnium
Notes
Like all synthetic elements, dubnium has no stable isotopes. The first isotope to be synthesized was 261Db in 1968. There are 13 known radioisotopes from 255Db to 270Db, and 1–3 isomers. The longest-lived known isotope is 268Db with a half-life of 29 hours.
