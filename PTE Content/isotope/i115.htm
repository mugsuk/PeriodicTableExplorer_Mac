Isotopes of Moscovium
Notes
Like all synthetic elements, moscovium has no stable isotopes. The first isotope to be synthesized was 288Mc in 2004. There are four known radioisotopes from 287Mc to 290Mc. The longest of these, 288Mc has a half-life of 87 milliseconds.
