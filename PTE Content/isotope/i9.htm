Isotopes of Fluorine
Notable Isotopes
18F [9 neutrons]
Abundance: synthetic
Half life: 109.77 minutes [ Electron Capture ]
Decay Energy: 1.656 MeV
Decays to 18O.
It is an important source of positrons, its primary use being in positron emission tomography (PET scans).
