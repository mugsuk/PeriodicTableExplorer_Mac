Isotopes of Flerovium
Notes
Like all synthetic elements, flerovium has no stable isotopes. The first isotope to be synthesized was 289Fl in 1999 (or possibly 1998). Flerovium has six known isotopes, and possibly 2 nuclear isomers. The longest-lived isotope is 289Fl with a half-life of 2.6 seconds.
