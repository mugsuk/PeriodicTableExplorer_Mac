Cameroon
Carbon (diamond)
In 2015, Cameroon produced approx. 5000 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2015 was 126.6m carats.
In 2009, Cameroon produced approx. 12000 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2009 was 121.3m carats.
Aluminium
2015 production of aluminium was approx. 65 000 tonnes. World production of aluminium in 2015 was 57.7m tonnes.
Cameroon's production of aluminium in 2009 was 73000 tonnes. World production of aluminium in 2009 was 36.9m tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Cameroon's mine production of gold was approx. 600 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Cameroon's mine production of gold was approx. 600 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Cameroon's mine production of gold was approx. 600 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
