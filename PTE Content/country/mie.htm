Ireland
Calcium
In 2015, Ireland's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 250 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Ireland's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 700 thousand tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Zinc
In 2015, Ireland produced 236 300 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 200 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Ireland produced 385 700 tonnes of zinc. World production in 2009 was 11.4m tonnes.
In 2006, Ireland produced around 425000 tonnes of zinc, around 4% of the world total.
Silver
In 2015, Ireland produced 3770 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
In 2009, Ireland produced 5.267 tonnes of silver. World production of silver in 2009 was 22236 tonnes.
Lead
In 2015, Ireland's production of refined lead was 17 200 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2015 was 10 700 000 tonnes.
In 2015, Ireland produced 31 300 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2015 was 5 million tonnes.
In 2009, Ireland's production of refined lead was 19 000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2006 was 8.9 million tonnes.
In 2006, Ireland produced 61800 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2006 was 3.5 million tonnes.
In 2006, Ireland's production of refined lead was 25 000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2006 was 8.1 million tonnes.
