Senegal
Zirconium
In 2015, Senegal produced 45248 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4). The total amount of zircon produced in 2015 was 1 341 000 tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Senegal's mine production of gold was 5670 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Senegal's mine production of gold was 5.655 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Senegal's mine production of gold was approx. 600 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2015, Senegal's production of refined lead was 1857 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2015 was 10 700 000 tonnes.
