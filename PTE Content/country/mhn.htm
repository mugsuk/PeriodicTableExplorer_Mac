Honduras
Calcium
In 2015, Honduras' production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 10 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Honduras' production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 60 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
