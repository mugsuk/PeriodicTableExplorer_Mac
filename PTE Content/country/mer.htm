Eritrea
Calcium
In 2015, Eritrea's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 1000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Eritrea's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 396 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Copper
In 2015, Eritrea's mine production of copper was 61600 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2015 was 19.2m tonnes.
Silver
In 2015, Eritrea produced 38 910 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
Gold
In 2015, Eritrea's mine production of gold was 753 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
