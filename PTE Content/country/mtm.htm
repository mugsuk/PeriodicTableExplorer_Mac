Turkmenistan
Bromine
In 2015, Turkmenistan produced approx. 500 tonnes of bromine. World production of bromine in 2015 was 569 000 tonnes.
Iodine
In 2015, Turkmenistan produced approx. 510 000 kg of iodine. World production of iodine in 2015 was 34 900 tonnes.
In 2009, Turkmenistan produced approx. 300 000 kg of iodine. World production of iodine in 2009 was 29200 tonnes.
In 2006, Turkmenistan produced approx. 270 000 kg of iodine, around 30% of the world total.
