Peru

Peru's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 394 289 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.

Peru's production of iron ore in 2006 was 5.885 million tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Production of pig iron in Peru in 2006 was 306000 tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.

Zinc
In 2015, Peru produced 1 422 000 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 200 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Peru produced 1.509m tonnes of zinc (3rd after the USA and Australia). World production in 2009 was 11.4m tonnes.
In 2006, Peru was the third largest producer of zinc, with around 1.2 million tonnes, about 11% of world total.

Mercury
More than 100 tons of mercury were mined from the region of Huancavelica, Peru, over the course of three centuries following the discovery of deposits there in 1563. The patio process and later pan amalgamation process continued to create great demand for mercury to treat silver ores until the late 1800s.
