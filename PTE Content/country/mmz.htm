Mozambique
Beryllium
In 2006, Mozambique produced 130 tonnes of beryl (Al2Be3Si6O18), one of the most important commercial compounds of beryllium. World production of beryl in 2006 was around 500 tonnes.
Aluminium
2015 production of aluminium was 558 400 tonnes. World production of aluminium in 2015 was 57.7m tonnes.
In 2009, Mozambique produced 544 700 tonnes of aluminium. Total world production was 36.9 million tonnes.
Aluminium (bauxite)
Mozambique's production of bauxite in 2015 was 4985 tonnes. World production of bauxite in 2015 was 294m tonnes.
Titanium
In 2015, Mozambique produced 763 500 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 6000 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
Zirconium
In 2015, Mozambique produced 51800 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4). The total amount of zircon produced in 2015 was 1 341 000 tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Mozambique's mine production of gold was 242 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Mozambique's mine production of gold was 511 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
Lead
In 2015, Mozambique's production of refined lead was 2494 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2015 was 10 700 000 tonnes.
