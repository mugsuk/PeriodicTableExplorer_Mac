New Caledonia
Cobalt
There is frequently a considerable disparity between the cobalt content of ore raised, and cobalt actually recovered.
In 2015, New Caledonia produced 11 210 tonnes of cobalt metal. Total world production of cobalt metal in 2015 was 148 000 tonnes.
In 2006, New Caledonia's mine production of cobalt was 1629 tonnes. otal world production of cobalt from mining, in 2006, was 53300 tonnes.
Nickel
In 2015, New Caledonia mined 186 065 400 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2015 was 2 092 000 tonnes.
In 2009, New Caledonia mined 92 570 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2009 was 1.412m tonnes.
New Caledonia's mine production of nickel in 2006 was 102986 tonnes. Worldwide mine production of nickel in 2006 was 1.526 million tonnes.
New Caledonia's combined smelter/refinery production of nickel in 2006 was 48723 tonnes. Worldwide smelter/refinery production of nickel in 2006 was 1.337 million tonnes.
