Israel
Magnesium
In 2015, Israel produced approx. 26 000 tonnes of magnesium metal. Total world production of magnesium metal was over 900 000 tonnes.
Calcium
In 2015, Israel's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 159 299 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Israel's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 110754 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Bromine
In 2015, Israel produced 115 583 tonnes of bromine. World production of bromine in 2015 was 569 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Israel produced 127 689 tonnes of bromine (3rd behind the USA and China). World production of bromine in 2009 was 520 thousand tonnes.
In 2006, Israel was the second largest producer, 179493 tonnes, world wide production was 570 thousand tonnes.
Israel and Jordan recover bromine from the bromine-rich brine wells from the Dead Sea waters, where bromine is found at up to 50000ppm.
Lead
In 2015, Israel's production of refined lead was 25 000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2015 was 10 700 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Israel's production of refined lead was 26 000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2009 was 8.9 million tonnes.
In 2006, Israel's production of refined lead was 25 000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2006 was 8.1 million tonnes.
