Gabon
Manganese
In 2015, Gabon produced approx. 4 million tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2015 was 53.2m tonnes.
In 2009, Gabon produced 2 million tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2009 was 33.4m tonnes.
Gabon has important manganese deposits.
Gold
In 2015, Gabon's mine production of gold was 1301 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Gabon's mine production of gold was approx. 300 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Gabon's mine production of gold was approx. 300 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
