Burkina Faso
Manganese
In 2015, Burkina Faso produced approx. 1600 tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2015 was 53.2m tonnes.
Zinc
In 2015, Burkina Faso produced 72 792 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 200 000 tonnes.
Silver
In 2015, Burkina Faso produced 29 000 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
Gold
In 2015, Burkina Faso's mine production of gold was 36 540 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Burkina Faso's mine production of gold was 13 181 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Burkina Faso's mine production of gold was 1571 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2015, Burkina Faso produced approx. 2000 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2015 was 5 million tonnes.
