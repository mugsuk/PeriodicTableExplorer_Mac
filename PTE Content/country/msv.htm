El Salvador
Calcium
El Salvador's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 6000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Lead
In 2009, El Salvador's production of refined lead was 12000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2009 was 8.9 million tonnes.
