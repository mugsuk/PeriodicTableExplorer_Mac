Uganda
Beryllium
In 2006, Uganda produced around 20 tonnes of beryl (Al2Be3Si6O18), one of the most important commercial compounds of beryllium. World production of beryl in 2006 was around 500 tonnes.
Iron
In 2015, Uganda's production of iron ore was 9000 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2015 was 3.328 billion tonnes.
Cobalt
There is frequently a considerable disparity between the cobalt content of ore raised, and cobalt actually recovered.
In 2015, Uganda produced zero tonnes of cobalt metal (and/or refined). Total world production of cobalt metal in 2015 was 148 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Uganda produced 673 tonnes of cobalt metal. Total world production of cobalt metal, in 2009, was 59300 tonnes.
In 2006, Uganda produced 689 tonnes of cobalt metal. Total world production of cobalt metal, in 2006, was 53300 tonnes.
Tin
In 2015, Uganda's mine production of tin was 135 tonnes. World production in 2015 was 341 000 tonnes.
In 2008, Uganda's mine production of tin was 40 tonnes. World production in 2008 was 273 thousand tonnes.
Tungsten
In 2015, Uganda produced 28 tonnes of tungsten. Total world production of tungsten in 2015 was 80 900 tonnes.
In 2009, Uganda produced approx. 50 tonnes of tungsten. Total world production of tungsten in 2006 was 74400 tonnes.
In 2006, Uganda produced 75 tonnes of tungsten. Total world production of tungsten in 2006 was 59100 tonnes.
Gold
In 2009, Uganda's mine production of gold was 931 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Uganda's mine production of gold was approx. 1.5 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
