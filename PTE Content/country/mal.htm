Albania
Calcium
In 2015, Albania's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 86 163 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Chromium
In 2015, Albania produced 639 909 tonnes of chromium ores and concentrates. Total worldwide production in 2015 was 35.3 million tonnes.
In 2006, Albania produced 201 120 tonnes of chromium ores and concentrates. Total worldwide production in 2006 was 21.2 million tonnes.
Nickel
In 2015, Albania mined 6280 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2015 was 2 092 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Albania mined 550 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2009 was 1.412m tonnes.
Copper
In 2015, Albania's mine production of copper was 1610 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2015 was 19.2m tonnes.
In 2009, Albania's mine production of copper was 2788 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2009 was 15.8m tonnes.
In 2006, Albania's production of copper by mining was 1140 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
