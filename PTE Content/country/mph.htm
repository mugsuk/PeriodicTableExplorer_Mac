Philippines

Cobalt
There is frequently a considerable disparity between the cobalt content of ore raised, and cobalt actually recovered.
In 2015, the Philippines produced 4140 tonnes of cobalt metal. Total world production of cobalt metal in 2015 was 148 000 tonnes.
Nickel
In 2015, the Philippines mined 416 019 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2015 was 2 092 000 tonnes.
In 2009, the Philippines mined 137 350 tonnes of nickel metal (4th behind Russia, Indonesia and Australia). World total nickel production by mining in 2009 was 1.412m tonnes.
Philippines's mine production of nickel in 2006 was 58900 tonnes. Worldwide mine production of nickel in 2006 was 1.526 million tonnes.
