French Guiana
Gold
In 2015, French Guiana's mine production of gold was 1265 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, French Guiana's mine production of gold was 1250 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, French Guiana's mine production of gold was approx. 3 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
