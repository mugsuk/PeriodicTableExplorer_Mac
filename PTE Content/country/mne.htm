Niger
Calcium
In 2015, Niger's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 253 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Niger's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 17500 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Silver
In 2015, Niger produced 119 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
In 2009, Niger produced approx. 300 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2009 was 22236 tonnes.
Tin
In 2015, Niger's mine production of tin was 8 tonnes. World production in 2015 was 341 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Niger's mine production of tin was approx. 10 tonnes. World production in 2009 was 279 thousand tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Niger's mine production of gold was 1209 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Niger's mine production of gold was 2.067 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Niger's mine production of gold was approx. 2.6 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Uranium
In 2015, Niger produced 4116 tonnes of uranium. World total production in 2015 was 60 500 tonnes.
In 2009, Niger produced 3241 tonnes of uranium (6th). World total production in 2009 was 50700 tonnes.
Niger was the fourth largest producer of uranium in 2006, 3434 tonnes, around 8.7% of the world total.
