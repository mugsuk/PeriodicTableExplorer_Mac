Ethiopia
Calcium
In 2015, Ethiopia's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 43 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Ethiopia's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 38809 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Silver

In 2009, Ethiopia produced 771 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2009 was 22236 tonnes.
