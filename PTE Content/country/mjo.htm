Jordan
Calcium
In 2015, Jordan's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 275 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Jordan's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 333710 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Bromine
In 2015, Jordan produced approx. 100 000 tonnes of bromine. World production of bromine in 2015 was 569 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Jordan produced 69 tonnes of bromine. World production of bromine in 2009 was 520 thousand tonnes.
In 2006, Jordan produced 94.5 tonnes, world wide production was 570 thousand tonnes.
Jordan and Israel recover bromine from the bromine-rich brine wells from the Dead Sea waters, where bromine is found at up to 50000ppm.
