Montenegro
Aluminium
2015 production of aluminium was 42 499 tonnes. World production of aluminium in 2015 was 57.7m tonnes.
In 2009, Montenegro produced 63600 tonnes of aluminium. Total world production was 36.9 million tonnes.
Aluminium (bauxite)
Montenegro's production of bauxite in 2015 was 50 369 tonnes. World production of bauxite in 2015 was 294m tonnes.
Zinc
In 2015, Montenegro produced 7068 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 200 000 tonnes.
Lead
In 2015, Montenegro produced 3476 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2015 was 5 million tonnes.
