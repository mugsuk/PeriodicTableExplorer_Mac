Tajikistan
Aluminium
2015 production of aluminium was 139 123 tonnes. World production of aluminium in 2015 was 57.7m tonnes.
In 2009, Tajikistan produced 359 400 tonnes of aluminium. Total world production was 36.9 million tonnes.
Calcium
In 2015, Tajikistan's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 8500 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Zinc
In 2015, Tajikistan produced 57 800 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 200 000 tonnes.
Silver
In 2015, Tajikistan produced approx. 2700 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
Antimony
In 2015, Tajikistan was the third largest producer at approx. 5400 tonnes. Total mine production of antimony in 2015 was 143 000 tonnes.
In 2005, Tajikistan was the fourth biggest producer of antimony, with around 2.7% (4073 tonnes) of the world total.
Gold
In 2015, Tajikistan's mine production of gold was approx. 4400 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Tajikistan's mine production of gold was 1361 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Tajikistan's mine production of gold was 1920 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Mercury
In 2015, Tajikistan produced approx. 100 000 kg of mercury. World production of mercury was 2 400 000 kg.
In 2006 it's estimated that Tajikistan produced 30 tonnes, around 2.5% of the world total.
Lead
In 2015, Tajikistan produced 31 000 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2015 was 5 million tonnes.
