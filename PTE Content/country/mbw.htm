Botswana
Carbon (Diamond)
In 2015, Botswana produced 20 824 000 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2015 was 126.6m carats.
In 2009, Botswana produced 17.734m carats of diamonds (third behind Russia and the DRC). Total production of diamonds in 2009 was 121.3m carats.
Botswana contains commercially viable diamond deposits. It is estimated that Botswana has reserves of around 225 million carats.
Cobalt
There is frequently a considerable disparity between the cobalt content of ore raised, and cobalt actually recovered.
In 2015, Botswana produced 316 tonnes of cobalt metal. Total world production of cobalt metal in 2015 was 148 000 tonnes.
In 2006, Botswana's mine production of cobalt was 303 tonnes. Total world production of cobalt metal, in 2006, was 53300 tonnes.
Nickel
In 2015, Botswana mined 16 789 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2015 was 2 092 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Botswana mined 29616 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2009 was 1.412m tonnes.
Botswana's mine production of nickel in 2006 was 26762 tonnes. Worldwide mine production of nickel in 2006 was 1.526 million tonnes.
Copper
In 2015, Botswana's mine production of copper was 22300 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2015 was 19.2m tonnes.
In 2009, Botswana's mine production of copper was 24382 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2009 was 15.8m tonnes.
In 2006, Botswana's production of copper by mining was 24255 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
Palladium
In 2015, Botswana produced 156 kg of palladium. World production of palladium in 2015 was 205 739 kg.
Silver
In 2015, Botswana produced 2801 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
Platinum
In 2015, Botswana produced 31 kg of platinum.
Gold
In 2015, Botswana's mine production of gold was 757 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Botswana's mine production of gold was 1.626 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Botswana's mine production of gold was 3.02 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
