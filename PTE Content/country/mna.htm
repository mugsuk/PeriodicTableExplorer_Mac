Namibia
Carbon (Diamond)
In 2015, Namibia produced 1 988 126 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2015 was 126.6m carats.
In 2009, Namibia produced 1.197m carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2009 was 121.3m carats.
Namibia produced 1.45 million carats of diamonds in 2003.
Calcium
In 2006, Namibia produced 132 249 tonnes of fluorspar (CaF2). World production was around 5.5 million tonnes.
Manganese
In 2015, Namibia produced 31 591 tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2015 was 53.2m tonnes.
In 2009, Namibia produced approx. 30000 tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2009 was 33.4m tonnes.
Copper
In 2015, Namibia's mine production of copper was 13913 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2015 was 19.2m tonnes.
In 2006, Namibia's production of copper by mining was 6262 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
Zinc
In 2015, Namibia produced 123 672 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 200 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Namibia produced 244 400 tonnes of zinc. World production in 2009 was 11.4m tonnes.
In 2006, Namibia produced around 425 000 tonnes of zinc, around 1.7% of the world total.
Arsenic
In 2015, Namibia produced 1960 tonnes of white arsenic (As2O3).
Silver
In 2015, Namibia produced 877 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
In 2009, Namibia produced 700 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2009 was 22236 tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Namibia's mine production of gold was 6105 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Namibia's mine production of gold was 2.022 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Namibia's mine production of gold was 2.79 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2015, Namibia produced 10 746 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2015 was 5 million tonnes.
In 2006, Namibia produced 11 830 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2006 was 3.5 million tonnes.
Uranium
In 2015, Namibia produced 2950 tonnes of uranium. World total production in 2015 was 60 500 tonnes.
In 2009, Namibia produced 4626 tonnes of uranium (4th). World total production in 2009 was 50700 tonnes.
Namibia was the sixth largest producer of uranium in 2006, 2782 tonnes, around 7% of the world total.
