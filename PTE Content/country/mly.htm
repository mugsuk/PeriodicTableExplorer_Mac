Libya
Calcium
In 2015, Libya's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 20 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Libya's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 175 thousand tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Iron
Production of pig iron in Libya in 2006 was 1.67 million tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
