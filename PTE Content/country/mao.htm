Angola
Carbon (diamond)
In 2015, Angola produced 9 016 343 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2015 was 126.6m carats.
In 2009, Angola produced 13.827m carats of diamonds (fourth behind Russia, DRC and Botswana). Total production of diamonds in 2009 was 121.3m carats.
Calcium
In 2015, Angola's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 190 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
