Malaysia
Magnesium
In 2015, Malaysia produced approx. 1600 tonnes of magnesium metal. Total world production of magnesium metal was over 900 000 tonnes.
Aluminium)
2015 production of aluminium was approx. 440 000 tonnes. World production of aluminium in 2015 was 57.7m tonnes.
Aluminium (bauxite)
Malaysia's production of bauxite in 2015 was approx. 27.7m tonnes (conservative British Geological Survey estimate based on reported exports). World production of bauxite in 2015 was 294m tonnes.
Titanium
In 2015, Malaysia produced 5814 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 198 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
In 2006, it produced 45649 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 16921 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
Manganese
In 2015, Malaysia produced 501 827 tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2015 was 53.2m tonnes.
In 2009, Malaysia produced 468 963 tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2009 was 33.4m tonnes.
Iron
In 2015, Malaysia's production of iron ore was 1 534 553 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2015 was 3.328 billion tonnes.
Malaysia's production of iron ore in 2006 was 667082 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Production of pig iron in Malaysia in 2006 was 1.5 million tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
Zirconium
In 2015, Malaysia produced 826 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4). The total amount of zircon produced in 2015 was 1 341 000 tonnes.
In 2006, Malaysia produced 1690 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4), the main commercial source of zirconium.
Silver
In 2015, Malaysia produced 945 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
In 2009, Malaysia produced 367 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2009 was 22236 tonnes.
Tin
In 2015, Malaysia's mine production of tin was 4158 tonnes. World production in 2015 was 341 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Malaysia's mine production of tin was 2412 tonnes. World production in 2009 was 279 thousand tonnes.
Malaysia's mine production of tin in 2006 was 2398 tonnes. World production in 2006 was 296 thousand tonnes.
Malaysia's smelter production of tin in 2006 was approx. 23000 tonnes. World smelter production in 2006 was 340 thousand tonnes.
Barium
In 2006, Malaysia produced 910 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2006 was 8.8 million tonnes.
Rare Earths
In 2015, Malaysia produced 365 tonnes of rare earth oxides.
In 2009 Malaysia produced 20 tonnes of rare earth oxides.
It is estimated that in 2006, Malaysia produced 580 tons of rare earth oxides.
Malaysia is estimated to have produced 700 tons of monazite in 2006. Monazite is an important ore for thorium, lanthanum, and cerium.
Gold
In 2015, Malaysia's mine production of gold was 4732 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Malaysia's mine production of gold was 2794 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Malaysia's mine production of gold was 3496 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Lead
In 2015, Malaysia's production of refined lead was 25 300 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2015 was 10 700 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Malaysia's production of refined lead was 54 000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2009 was 8.9 million tonnes.
In 2006, Malaysia's production of refined lead was 44 000 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2006 was 8.1 million tonnes.
Thorium
It's believed that Malaysia has reserves of around 4.5 tonnes of monazite, the primary thorium mineral.
