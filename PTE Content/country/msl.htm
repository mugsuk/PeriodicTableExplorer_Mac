Sierra Leone
Carbon (diamond)
In 2015, Sierra Leone produced 500 050 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2015 was 126.6m carats.
In 2009, Sierra Leone produced 400480 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2009 was 121.3m carats.
Aluminium (bauxite)
Sierra Leone's production of bauxite in 2015 was 1.334046m tonnes. World production of bauxite in 2015 was 294m tonnes.
Titanium
In 2015, Sierra Leone produced 48 420 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and approx. 123 000 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
In 2006, Sierra Leone produced approx. 13800 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 73600 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
Iron
In 2015, Sierra Leone's production of iron ore was approx. 7 000 000 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2015 was 3.328 billion tonnes.
Zirconium
In 2015, Sierra Leone produced approx. 1000 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4). The total amount of zircon produced in 2015 was 1 341 000 tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Sierra Leone's mine production of gold was 87 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Sierra Leone's mine production of gold was 143 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
