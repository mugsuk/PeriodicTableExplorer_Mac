Kyrgyzstan
Calcium
In 2006, Kyrgyzstan produced 4000 tonnes of fluorspar (CaF2). World production was around 5.5 million tonnes.
Copper
In 2015, Kyrgyzstan's mine production of copper was 3100 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2015 was 19.2m tonnes.
Molybdenum
In 2009, Kyrgyzstan mined approx. 250 tonnes of molybdenum. World mine production in 2009 was 231000 tonnes.
Antimony
In 2015, Kyrgyzstan mined approx. 2000 tonnes of antimony. Total mine production of antimony in 2015 was 143 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Kyrgyzstan mined 918 tonnes of antimony. Total mine production of antimony was 179000 tonnes.
Tungsten
In 2015, Kyrgyzstan produced approx. 100 tonnes of tungsten. Total world production of tungsten in 2015 was 80 900 tonnes.
In 2009, Kyrgyzstan produced approx. 100 tonnes of tungsten, as scheelite (CaWO4). Total world production of tungsten in 2009 was 62300 tonnes.
In 2006, Kyrgyzstan produced approx. 100 tonnes of tungsten. Total world production of tungsten in 2006 was 59100 tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Kyrgyzstan's mine production of gold was 17 600 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Kyrgyzstan's mine production of gold was approx. 17 200 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Kyrgyzstan's mine production of gold was 10 301 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
Mercury
In 2015, Kyrgyzstan produced 20 000 kg of mercury. World production of mercury was 2 400 000 kg.
In 2009, Kyrgyzstan produced approx. 250 tonnes of mercury (second after China). World prodcution of mercury was 1700 tonnes.
Kyrgyzstan was the second largest producer of mercury in 2006, 250 tonnes, 28.5% of the world total.
