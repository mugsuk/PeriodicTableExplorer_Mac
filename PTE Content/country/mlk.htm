Sri Lanka
Carbon (graphite)
In 2015, Sri Lanka produced approx. 3100 tonnes of graphite. World production in 2015 was 2 200 000 tonnes.
Sri Lanka produced 3200 tonnes in 2006 and 3000 tonnes in 2005 of "lump" or "vein" graphite.
Calcium
In 2015, Sri Lanka's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 600 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Titanium
In 2015, Sri Lanka produced 39 439 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 1808 of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
In 2006, Sri Lanka produced 57034 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 2280 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
Zirconium
In 2015, Sri Lanka produced 641 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4). The total amount of zircon produced in 2015 was 1 341 000 tonnes.
In 2006, Sri Lanka produced 8321 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4), the main commercial source of zirconium.
Lead
In 2015, Sri Lanka's production of refined lead was 2548 tonnes. Total world production of refined lead in 2015 was 10 700 000 tonnes.
