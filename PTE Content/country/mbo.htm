Bolivia
Lithium
The most important deposit of lithium, with around 50% of the world's reserves, are to be found in the Salar de Uyuni area of Bolivia.
Calcium
In 2015, Bolivia's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 650 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Iron
In 2015, Bolivia's production of iron ore was 24 453 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2015 was 3.328 billion tonnes.
Copper
In 2015, Bolivia's mine production of copper was 9480 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2015 was 19.2m tonnes.
In 2009, Bolivia's mine production of copper was 620 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2009 was 15.8m tonnes.
Zinc
In 2015, Bolivia produced 442 154 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 200 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Bolivia produced 430 879 tonnes of zinc. World production in 2009 was 11.4m tonnes.
Arsenic
In 2015, Bolivia produced 38 tonnes of white arsenic (As2O3).
In 2006, Bolivia produced 120 tonnes of white arsenic (As2O3).
Silver
In 2015, Bolivia produced 1 306 098 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
In 2009, Bolivia produced 1325.73 tonnes of silver. World production of silver in 2009 was 22236 tonnes.
Tin
In 2015, Bolivia's mine production of tin was 20 135 tonnes. World production in 2015 was 341 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Bolivia's mine production of tin was 19581 tonnes. World production in 2009 was 279 thousand tonnes.
Bolivia's mine production of tin in 2006 was 17669 tonnes. World production in 2006 was 296 thousand tonnes.
Bolivia's smelter production of tin in 2006 was approx. 13500 tonnes. World smelter production in 2006 was 340 thousand tonnes.
Antimony
In 2015, Bolivia mined 3 843 tonnes of antimony. Total mine production of antimony in 2015 was 143 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Bolivia mined 2990 tonnes of antimony (2nd place behind Russia). Total mine production of antimony was 179000 tonnes.
In 2005, Bolivia was the third biggest producer of antimony, with around 3.5% (5226 tonnes) of the world total.

In 2006, Bolivia produced 8943 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2006 was 8.8 million tonnes.
