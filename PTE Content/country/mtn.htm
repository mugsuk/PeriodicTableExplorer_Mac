Tunisia
Calcium
In 2015, Tunisia's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was approx. 630 000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Tunisia's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 115000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Iron
In 2015, Tunisia's production of iron ore was 300 000 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2015 was 3.328 billion tonnes.
Tunisia's production of iron ore in 2006 was 217300 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Zinc
In 2014, Tunisia produced 7400 tonnes of zinc from mining. World mine production of zinc in 2015 was 13 700 000 tonnes.
Barium
In 2015, Tunisia produced approx. 10000 tonnes of barytes (BaSO4), the main source of barium. World production of barytes in 2015 was 7.9 million tonnes.
Lead
In 2006, Tunisia produced 1000 tonnes of lead from mining. The total amount of lead mined in 2006 was 3.5 million tonnes.
