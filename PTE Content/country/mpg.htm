Papua New Guinea
Cobalt
There is frequently a considerable disparity between the cobalt content of ore raised, and cobalt actually recovered.
In 2015, Papua New Guinea produced 2505 tonnes of cobalt metal. Total world production of cobalt metal in 2015 was 148 000 tonnes.
Nickel
In 2015, Papua New Guinea mined 25 582 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2015 was 2 092 000 tonnes.
Copper
In 2015, Papua New Guinea's mine production of copper was 45 185 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2015 was 19.2m tonnes.
In 2009, Papua New Guinea's mine production of copper was 166 669 tonnes. Total world production of mined copper in 2009 was 15.8m tonnes.
In 2006, Papua New Guinea's production of copper by mining was 194 355 tonnes, total world production was 15.1 million tonnes.
Silver
In 2015, Papua New Guinea produced 44 588 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
In 2009, Papua New Guinea produced 48 062 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2009 was 21.683 tonnes.
Gold
In 2015, Papua New Guinea's mine production of gold was 12 687 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Papua New Guinea's mine production of gold was 67 800 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Papua New Guinea's mine production of gold was 58 349 k. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
