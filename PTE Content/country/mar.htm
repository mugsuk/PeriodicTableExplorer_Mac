Argentina
Lithium
In 2015, Argentina produced approx. 19 000 tonnes of lithium minerals. Lithium content from total world production of lithium minerals in 2015 was 26 200 tonnes.
In 2009, Argentina produced 5016 tonnes of lithium minerals.
Argentina is currently the second largest producer of lithium in the world, where it is recovered from brine pools.
Argentina has deposits of spodumene (the most important of the lithium ores) LiAlSi2O6.
