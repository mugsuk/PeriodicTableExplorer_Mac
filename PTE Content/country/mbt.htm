Bhutan
Calcium
In 2015, Bhutan's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 389 365 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Bhutan's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 160000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Iron
In 2015, Bhutan's production of iron ore was 43202 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2015 was 3.328 billion tonnes.
Bhutan's production of iron ore in 2006 was approx. 5300 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
