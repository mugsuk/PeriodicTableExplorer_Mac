Scandium
The Iveland-Evje Region in Norway and Madagascar are the only locations where minable deposits of minerals with high scandium content, thortveitite (Y,Sc)2(Si2O7) and kolbeckite (ScPO4.2H2O).

Molybdenum
In 2015, Norway mined 1 tonne of molybdenum. World mine production of molybdenum in 2015 was 292 000 tonnes.
The Knaben mine in southern Norway was opened in 1885, making it the first molybdenum mine. It remained open until 1973.
