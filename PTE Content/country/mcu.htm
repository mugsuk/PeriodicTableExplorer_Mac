Cuba
Calcium
In 2015, Cuba's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 90 900 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Cuba's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 110000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Chromium
In 2006, Cuba produced approx. 34 thousand tonnes of chromium ores and concentrates. Total worldwide production in 2006 was 21.2 million tonnes.
Iron
Cuba's production of iron ore in 2006 was 7800 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Cobalt
There is frequently a considerable disparity between the cobalt content of ore raised, and cobalt actually recovered.
In 2015, Cuba produced 3734 tonnes of cobalt metal. Total world production of cobalt metal in 2015 was 148 000 tonnes.
In 2006, Cuba's mine production of cobalt was approx. 4000 tonnes. Total world production of cobalt from mining, in 2006, was 53300 tonnes.
Nickel
In 2015, Cuba mined 56 400 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2015 was 2 092 000 tonnes.
In 2009, Cuba mined 65 000 tonnes of nickel metal. World total nickel production by mining in 2009 was 1.412m tonnes.
Cuba's mine production of nickel in 2006 was 74000 tonnes. Worldwide mine production of nickel in 2006 was 1.526 million tonnes.
Cuba's combined smelter/refinery production of nickel in 2006 was 39100 tonnes. Worldwide smelter/refinery production of nickel in 2006 was 1.337 million tonnes.
Gold
