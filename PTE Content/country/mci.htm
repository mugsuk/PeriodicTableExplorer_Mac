Ivory Coast
Carbon (diamond)
In 2015, Ivory Coast produced 14 925 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2015 was 126.6m carats.
In 2009, Ivory Coast produced approx. 300000 carats of diamonds. Total production of diamonds in 2009 was 121.3m carats.
Manganese
In 2015, the Ivory Coast produced approx. 250 000 tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2015 was 53.2m tonnes.
In 2009, the Ivory Coast produced 150000 tonnes of manganese ore. World production of manganese ore in 2009 was 33.4m tonnes.
Silver
In 2015, the Ivory Coast produced 587 kg of silver. World production of silver in 2015 was 27 511 000 kg.
Gold
In 2015, the Ivory Coast's mine production of gold was 20 245 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, the Ivory Coast's mine production of gold was 6.947 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, the Ivory Coast's mine production of gold was approx. 1.3 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
