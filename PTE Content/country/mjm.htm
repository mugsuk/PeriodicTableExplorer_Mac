Jamaica
Aluminium (bauxite)
Jamaica has large deposits of bauxite, aluminium ore, and is one of the primary mining countries.
Jamaica's production of bauxite in 2015 was 1.724162 tonnes. World production of bauxite in 2015 was 294m tonnes.
Calcium
In 2015, Jamaica's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) was 42 883 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2015 was 268.2 million tonnes.
Jamaica's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was 364432 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
