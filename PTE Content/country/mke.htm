Kenya
Calcium

In 2006, Kenya produced 132 030 tonnes of fluorspar (CaF2). World production was around 5.5 million tonnes.
Kenya's production of gypsum (CaSO4.2H2O) in 2006 was approx. 11000 tonnes. World production of gypsum in 2006 was 142.5 millions tonnes.
Titanium
In 2015, Kenya produced 444 999 tonnes of ilmenite (FeTiO3), and 78 947 tonnes of rutile (TiO2), the only two commercially important titanium ores.
Zirconium
In 2015, Kenya produced 25951 tonnes of zircon (ZrSiO4). The total amount of zircon produced in 2015 was 1 341 000 tonnes.
