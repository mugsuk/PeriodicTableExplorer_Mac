Uruguay
Gold
In 2015, Uruguay's mine production of gold was 1480 kg. Total amount of gold mined in 2015 was 3110 tonnes.
In 2009, Uruguay's mine production of gold was 2.01 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2009 was 2460 tonnes.
In 2006, Uruguay's mine production of gold was approx. 2.8 tonnes. Total amount of gold mined in 2006 was 2310 tonnes.
