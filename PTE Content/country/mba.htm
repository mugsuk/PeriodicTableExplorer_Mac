Iron

Bosnia and Herzegovina's production of iron ore in 2006 was 3 439 587 tonnes. Total world production of iron ore in 2006 was 1.81 billion tonnes.
Production of pig iron in Bosnia and Herzegovina in 2006 was approx. 60 000 tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
