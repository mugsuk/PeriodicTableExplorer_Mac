Qatar
Helium
In 2004-2006 a plant in Ras Laffen was built. As of early 2007, Ras Laffen is functioning at 50%. Current output is around 13 million cubic km, which is predicted to rise to 14 million cubic km by 2030.
Aluminium
2015 production of aluminium was 637 900 tonnes. World production of aluminium in 2015 was 57.7m tonnes.
Iron
Production of pig iron in Qatar in 2006 was 876885 tonnes. Total world production of pig iron in 2006 was 925.8 million tonnes.
