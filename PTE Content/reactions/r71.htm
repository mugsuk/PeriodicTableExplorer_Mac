Reactions of Lutetium
Reactions with water
Lutetium is quite electropositive and reacts slowly with cold water and quite quickly with hot water to form lutetium hydroxide and hydrogen gas.
  2Lu(s) + 6H2O(g)2Lu(OH)3(aq) + 3H2(g)
Reactions with air
Lutetium metal tarnishes slowly in air and burns readily to form lutetium (III) oxide.
  4Lu + 3O22Lu2O3
Reactions with halogens
Lutetium metal reacts with all the halogens to form lutetium(III) halides.
  2Lu(s) + 3F2(g)2LuF3(s)
  2Lu(s) + 3Cl2(g)2LuCl3(s)
  2Lu(s) + 3Br2(g)2LuBr3(s)
  2Lu(s) + 3I2(g)2LuI3(s)
Reactions with acids
Lutetium dissolves readily in dilute sulphuric acid to form solutions containing the colourless aquated Lu(III) ion together with hydrogen gas.
  2Lu(s) + 3H2SO4(aq)2Lu3+(aq) + SO42-(aq) + 3H2(g)
