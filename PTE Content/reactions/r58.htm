Reactions of Cerium
Reactions with water
Cerium is quite electropositive and reacts slowly with cold water and quite quickly with hot water to form cerium hydroxide and hydrogen gas.
  2Ce(s) + 6H2O(g)2Ce(OH)3(aq) + 3H2(g)
Reactions with air
Cerium tarnishes slowly in air and burns readily to form cerium (IV) oxide.
  Ce + O2CeO2
Reactions with halogens
Cerium metal reacts with all the halogens to form cerium(III) halides.
  2Ce(s) + 3F2(g)2CeF3(s)
  2Ce(s) + 3Cl2(g)2CeCl3(s)
  2Ce(s) + 3Br2(g)2CeBr3(s)
  2Ce(s) + 3I2(g)2CeI3(s)
Reactions with acids
Cerium dissolves readily in dilute sulphuric acid to form solutions containing the colourless aquated Ce(III) ion together with hydrogen gas.
  2Ce(s) + 3H2SO4(aq)2Ce3+(aq) + 3SO42-(aq) + 3H2(g)
