Reactions of Europium
Reactions with water
Europium is quite electropositive and reacts slowly with cold water and fairly quickly with hot water to form europium hydroxide, and hydrogen gas.
  2Eu(s) + 6H2O(g)2Eu(OH)3(aq) + 3H2(g)
Reactions with air
Europium metal burns readily in air to form europium(III) oxide.
  4Eu + 3O22Eu2O3
Reactions with halogens
Europium metal reacts with all the halogens to form europium(III) halides.
  2Eu(s) + 3F2(g)2EuF3(s)
  2Eu(s) + 3Cl2(g)2EuCl3(s)
  2Eu(s) + 3Br2(g)2EuBr3(s)
  2Eu(s) + 3I2(g)2EuI3(s)
Reactions with acids
Europium metal dissolves readily in dilute sulphuric acid to form solutions containing the very pale pink Eu(III) ion together with hydrogen gas.
  2Eu(s) + 3H2SO4(aq)2Eu3+(aq) + 3SO42-(aq) + 3H2(g)
