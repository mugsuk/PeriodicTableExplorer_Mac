Reactions of Xenon
Reactions with water
Xenon does not react with water.
Reactions with air
Xenon does not react with air.
Reactions with halogens
At a pressure of 6 atmospheres, xenon and fluorine will react. There are currently no reports of other halogens reacting with xenon.
  Xe(s) + F2(g)XeF2(s)
  Xe(s) + 2F2(g)XeF4(s)
  Xe(s) + 3F2(g)XeF6(s)
Reactions with acids
Xenon does not react with acids.
Reactions with bases
Xenon does not react with bases.
