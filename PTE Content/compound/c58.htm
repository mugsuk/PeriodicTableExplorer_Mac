Cerium Compounds
   Ammonium cerium(IV) nitrate
   Cerium(IV) oxide
Ammonium cerium(IV) nitrate (NH4)2Ce(NO3)6
An important component of Chrome etchant, a material that is used in the production of liquid crystal displays (LCDs).
Cerium(IV) oxide CeO2
It is used in ceramics, to polish glass, and to sensitize photosensitive glass. It is also used in the walls of self-cleaning ovens.
