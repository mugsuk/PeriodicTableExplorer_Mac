Mercury Compounds
   Mercury(II) fulminate
   Mercury(II) sulfate
Mercury(II) fulminate Hg(ONC)2
: Highly Toxic :
It is highly sensitive to friction and shock. It is mainly used in blasting caps.
Mercury(II) sulfate HgSO4
Mercury sulfate is used as a catalyst in the production of acetaldehyde from acetylene and water. Methyl mercury is produced as a side product of this conversion. Mercury-containing pollutants from this reaction are held to have caused Minamata disease in Minamata, Japan.
