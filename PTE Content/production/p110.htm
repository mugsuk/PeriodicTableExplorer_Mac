Occurrence and Production of Darmstadtium
Synthesis
Darmstadtium was first created on November 9, 1994, at the Institute for Heavy Ion Research (Gesellschaft für Schwerionenforschung, GSI) in Darmstadt, Germany, by Peter Armbruster and Gottfried Münzenberg, under the direction of Sigurd Hofmann.
Darmstadtium was created by bombarding lead-208 with nickel-62 atoms; by bombarding lead-208 with nickel-64 atoms; or by observing the decay of heavier elements.
