Occurrence and Production of Oganesson
Synthesis
The element is created by fusing together californium and calcium atoms.
On 9 October 2006, the researchers announced that they had indirectly detected a total of three (possibly four) nuclei of oganesson-294 (one or two in 2002 and two more in 2005) produced via collisions of californium-249 atoms and calcium-48 ions.
