Occurrence and Production of Meitnerium
Synthesis
Meitnerium was first created on August 29, 1982, at the Institute for Heavy Ion Research (Gesellschaft für Schwerionenforschung, GSI) in Darmstadt, Germany, by Peter Armbruster and Gottfried Münzenberg.
Meitnerium is created by bombarding bismuth-209 with iron-58 nuclei.
During the first experiment to synthesize meitnerium, a single atom of meitnerium-266 was detected.
