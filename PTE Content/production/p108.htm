Occurrence and Production of Hassium
Synthesis
Hassium was first created in 1984, at the Institute for Heavy Ion Research (Gesellschaft für Schwerionenforschung, GSI) in Darmstadt, Germany, by Peter Armbruster and Gottfried Münzenberg.
Hassium is created by bombarding lead-208 with iron-58 nuclei.
During the first experiment to synthesize hassium, three atoms of hassium-265 were detected.
