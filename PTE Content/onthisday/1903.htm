March 19th
1900 Frédéric J. Joliot (Joliot-Curie), Nobel Prize winner in 1935 with wife Irène Joliot-Curie, for production of artificial radioisotopes, was born. He proved experimentally that neutron emission occurs in nuclear fission with H. Halban and L. W. Kowarski.
1883 Walter N. Haworth, who synthesized ascorbic acid (Vitamin C), was born. He was researcher on sugars and dextran as blood plasma substitute; Nobel Prize (1937) winner for vitamin synthesis, with Paul Karrer.
