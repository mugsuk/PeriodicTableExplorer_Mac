February 2nd
1802 Jean Baptiste Joseph Dieudonne Boussingault, who demonstrated that plants absorb nitrogen from soil in form of nitrates and not from air as previously believed; isolated and named sorbitol, was born.
1566 Michal Sedziwój (Michael Sendivogius), was born. He was an alchemist; developed methods for isolating and purifying various acids, metals and other chemical compounds; discovered that air is not a single substance and contains a life-giving substance; identified this 'food of life' with the gas given off by heating nitre (saltpetre).
